# slayr Features

## 🎯 Core Features

### 1. AI-Powered Outfit Analysis
Upload any outfit photo and receive instant AI-driven insights powered by Google Gemini Vision.

**What you get:**
- Fashion score (1-100)
- Aesthetic classification
- Vibe description
- Analysis in 5-10 seconds

### 2. Clothing Detection
Advanced computer vision identifies individual clothing pieces.

**Detects:**
- Item type (blazer, shirt, pants, etc.)
- Color description
- Style details
- Fit characteristics

### 3. Aesthetic Classification
Categorizes your outfit into one of 10 fashion aesthetics.

**Categories:**
- **Streetwear** - Urban, edgy, sneaker-focused
- **Old Money** - Timeless luxury, understated elegance
- **Clean Girl** - Fresh, minimal, neutral tones
- **Y2K** - Early 2000s nostalgia
- **Dark Academia** - Scholarly vintage romance
- **Minimal Luxury** - Sophisticated simplicity
- **Techwear** - Functional futurism
- **Vintage** - Retro-inspired classics
- **Scandinavian Minimal** - Nordic sustainability
- **Korean Casual** - K-fashion trends

### 4. Color Palette Analysis
Extracts dominant colors from your outfit.

**Features:**
- Hex color codes
- Color names
- Dominance percentages
- Visual swatches

### 5. Smart Recommendations
Personalized styling tips from AI fashion expert.

**Includes:**
- What's working well
- How to enhance the look
- Balance suggestions
- Proportion tips

### 6. Accessory Suggestions
AI recommends accessories to complete your outfit.

**Suggests:**
- Jewelry
- Bags
- Shoes
- Watches
- Belts
- Hats

### 7. Fashion Tags
Auto-generated descriptive tags for your style.

**Examples:**
- minimal
- luxury
- edgy
- professional
- casual
- timeless

## 💡 Pro Tips

### Best Photos for Analysis

**Good:**
- Full body shots
- Good lighting
- Plain background
- Clear details
- Frontal angle

**Avoid:**
- Dark/blurry photos
- Extreme angles
- Busy backgrounds
- Cropped views
- Filters

### Getting Better Scores

1. **Color Coordination** - Match your palette
2. **Proper Fit** - Tailored pieces score higher
3. **Proportion** - Balance tops and bottoms
4. **Cohesion** - Stick to one aesthetic
5. **Details** - Accessories make a difference

### Maximizing Insights

- Upload different angles
- Try various aesthetics
- Compare similar outfits
- Track improvements
- Experiment boldly
